Golem Smash!

Golem Smash is a 2D platformer where you use your skills as an archer to defeat a golem.
The golem will try to defeat you too, so be careful!

Controls:
Use W and D to move left and right!
Press the Space Bar to jump!
Hold S to drop through platforms!
Click and drag with the left mouse button to draw your bow, and release the mouse to fire!
Choose your arrow types with 1, 2, and 3:
Regular arrows damage the Golem
Light torches with fire arrows to increase your damage
Stun the Golem with ice arrows!
